--- layout: post title: 'Arxiv trackbacks' date: 2005-08-24 21:51:00 tags: [blogging, papers] --- Good news from ars mathematica: arxiv now supports trackbacks, with the aid of which one can make links from blog entries bidirectional. Bad news, though, livejournal doesn't support them, and more bad news, the trackback protocol doesn't look like something that could be easily faked up just by coding one's URLs differently and allowing referrer autodiscovery to do its magic, or anything like that. But good news, there are third party tools to handle the trackback protocol for you. Maybe I'll try using one of these tools to trackback my entries here to the papers they refer to, and see how it works...but this looks cumbersome enough that I'm not going to bother to do it every time I link to someone else's blog postings.
