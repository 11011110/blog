--- layout: post title: 'Useful bibliographic search site of the day' date: 2008-08-15 18:21:00 tags: [tools, papers] --- Crossref.org. You give it author and title, it gives you other bibliographic details including a DOI (the part I was looking for; basically a more-permanent version of the publisher's official web address for the paper). The link is to their "guest query" page; they also have a different interface for batch queries for registered users, but I think that's not intended for individuals.
