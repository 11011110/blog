--- layout: post title: 'Ambigrams' date: 2005-08-19 10:43:00 tags: [typography] --- Metafilter just linked to ambigram matic, an automated web application for writing Roman-alphabet text so that it reads the same upside down. Or alternatively so that it has a second different reading upside down. It can only invert pairs of words with the same number of letters; obviously the method is to have a cross-table of upside-down pairs for each pair of Roman letters. The results are a little unaesthetic compared to Scott Kim's inversions, or even Scott Kim's quicky sketches, but what do you expect?
