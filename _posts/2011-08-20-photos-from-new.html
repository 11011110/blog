--- layout: post title: 'Photos from New York' date: 2011-08-20 11:40:00 tags: [art, photography] --- What, nearly six days in New York City and I only brought back five photos? Well, no, actually there's another set that I haven't processed yet. The ones in this set include a couple from MoMA, one of the Statue of Liberty from the WADS dinner cruise, and a couple from the Brooklyn Heights Promenade.



There would also have been photos of the Lee Ufan exhibit and of Hans-Peter Feldmann's room wallpapered with 100,000 $1 bills from the Guggenheim if only they allowed photography. It's a lot smaller of a museum than MoMA but definitely worth seeing.
