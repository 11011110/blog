--- layout: post title: 'Paper permutohedron' date: 2009-12-04 09:16:00 tags: [xyz graphs, permutohedron, origami] --- Make your own paper orthogonal permutohedron! Here's how. Print out the pattern, cut the solid lines, valley fold the dashed lines, and mountain fold the dash-dot lines. (I used a box-cutter both to make the cuts and to prescore the folds.)
