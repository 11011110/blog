The list of accepted papers and abstracts from COCOON 2012 is online.

Quite a few of them are about graph drawing and geometric graphs:
  • Geometric RAC simultaneous drawings of graphs, by Evmorfia Argyriou, Michael Bekos, Michael Kaufmann and Antonios Symvonis
  • Monotone paths in planar convex subdivisions, by Adrian Dumitrescu, Günter Rote and Csaba Toth
  • Simultaneous embeddings with vertices mapping to pre-specified points, by Taylor Gordon
  • Multilevel drawings of clustered graphs, by Fabrizio Frati
  • Outerplanar graph drawings with few slopes, by Kolja Knauer, Piotr Micek and Bartosz Walczak
  • Fáry's theorem for 1-planar graphs, by Seok-Hee Hong, Peter Eades, Giuseppe Liotta and S Poon
  • Unexplored Steiner ratios in geometric networks, by Lilach Chaitman-Yerushalmi and Paz Carmi
