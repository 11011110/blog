--- layout: post title: 'Russian Gulch' date: 2009-08-29 20:34:00 tags: [mendocino, photography, landscape, family] --- After Southern Oregon we took a drive down the north coast of California to visit my parents in Mendocino and see my mother give a reading from her newly published poetry book (from March Street Press). Before Diana and I left the kids and took off for the Canadian Rockies, we had some time for a family hike, and I pushed for Russian Gulch State Park, in part so that I could take a photo of the bridge that now adorns the Wikipedia article on the park.
