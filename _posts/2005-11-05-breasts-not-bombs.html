--- layout: post title: 'Breasts not bombs' date: 2005-11-05 11:26:00 tags: [free speech, photography] --- Heh. The topless peace protesters I photographed in this year's Mendocino July 4th parade have made The LA Times and Fark after being prevented from doing their thing again in Sacramento. Apparently, according to the judge, "being topless is not inherently expressive". Which I'm happy to agree with — sometimes it could just be for the purpose of getting a nice even tan — except that I'd expect the standard for whether this counts as free speech to be, not whether it's necessarily expressive in all cases, but whether it's expressive in this case. Which it clearly seems to be. I suppose there's a reason I'm not a lawyer...
