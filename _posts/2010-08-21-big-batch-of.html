I just returned from visiting family in Northern California, and just uploaded several batches of photos from the visit: some family snapshots of my cousins and their kids at the San Francisco zoo; walking across the Golden Gate bridge with my son; the Baxman Gravel Company, a beautifully decrepit (though still functional) gravel and concrete factory on the coast north of Fort Bragg; and some photos of various places along the Mendocino County coast, mostly taken with the intention of illustrating some of the related Wikipedia articles.
