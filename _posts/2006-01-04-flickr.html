Mike G. persuaded me to get a flickr account. So now you can look at a small fraction of my photos, selected for their non-snapshotness, and comment on them or do whatever other things flickr might let its users do with photos. Maybe even play Sudoku with them. I'm not really expecting it to be any better at leading to useful feedback on the photos than photo.net wasn't, but maybe at least a few more people will find them that way.
