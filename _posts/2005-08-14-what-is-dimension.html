--- layout: post title: 'What is the dimension of the set of partitions?' date: 2005-08-14 11:03:00 tags: [partitions, media theory, number theory] ---

The lattice dimension of the partial cube of partitions of n is Θ(sqrt n).

For the lower bound, consider how many neighbors the triangular partition 1+2+3+... has.

For the upper bound, assign to each partition sumi > 0 xi, xi > xi+1, the coordinates (x1, x2, ..., xsqrt n, y1, y2, ..., ysqrt n), where yj denotes the number of values greater than or equal to j among the xi with i > sqrt n.
