--- layout: post title: 'Thunderbird' date: 2005-11-18 21:38:00 tags: [tools] --- I'm trying Thunderbird as my new email client — previously I'd been using Mulberry, as Apple Mail couldn't cope with the huge number of folders on my imap server, and previous OS X versions of Thunderbird I'd tried some time ago had difficulties running at all. But Mulberry's maker has gone out of business, I had an old unsupported version, and it was very slow to open. So far I like what I see from T-bird. It's fast, it looks good, the price is right, and it seems to work pretty well. Unless I run into some serious problem, I doubt I'm going back.
