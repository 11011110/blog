--- layout: post title: 'A brief history of curves in graph drawing' date: 2013-04-08 21:34:00 tags: [graph drawing, conferences, talks] --- I'm in Dagstuhl this week for a workshop on Drawing Graphs and Maps with Curves. I gave a talk today surveying the use of curved edges in graph drawing, which may be older than you might think. For instance, did you know that arc diagrams, named from a 2002 paper by Wattenberg in information visualization, actually go back to two papers from the 1960s on crossing minimization?
