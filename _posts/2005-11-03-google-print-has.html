--- layout: post title: 'Google Print has an early success' date: 2005-11-03 14:22:00 tags: [books, information retrieval, geometry] --- The first story I've seen about successful use of Google Print, and it's a recreational geometry book. Eric Gjerde used Google Print to find Greg Frederickson's book Hinged Dissections: Swinging and Twisting. He writes: "I can honestly say I would never have found this book if it was not indexed in Google Print. "
