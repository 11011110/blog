--- layout: post title: 'Unscientific' date: 2007-01-24 18:12:00 tags: [open access, academia, papers] --- Scientific publishers launch disinformation campaign against open-access scientific publication. Specifically, they don't want grant agencies to require researchers to make their results public, so they're calling any such requirement "censorship". Newspeak at its best. Sadly, it's not just the usual scam artistscommercial scientific publishing houses such as Elsevier and Wiley that are involved: it's also the American Chemical Society. Another great reason to take one's own papers to journals not controlled by these groups. Via janitor_x.
