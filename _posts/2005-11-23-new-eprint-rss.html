--- layout: post title: 'New eprint RSS feeds' date: 2005-11-23 23:24:00 tags: [cryptography, graph drawing, papers] --- Good news if you want to pay attention to graph drawing research: the Graph Drawing Epreint Archive (GDEA) now has an RSS feed. As (via ephermata) does the International Association for Cryptographic Research (IACR) eprint archive.

Not sure how I feel about the proliferation of specialized archives (as opposed to more comprehensive ones like arxiv) but this will make it a lot easier to keep up with them.
