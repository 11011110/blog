--- layout: post title: 'Warning re FOCS URLs' date: 2009-04-30 10:25:00 tags: [conferences] --- A warning, passed along from Phil Klein: if you have any old web pages lying around with URLs pointing to focs.org or focs****.org, you should change them to the links listed in Wikipedia's page on FOCS. It seems the .org domains have been taken over by domain-name spammers.
