Last week, following my trip to Dagstuhl, I was in the Netherlands for a research visit and a talk on Lombardi drawing at the 7th Dutch Computational Geometry Day. But while there I also found some time to see and photograph some of the stereotypical Dutch architectural forms: canals, windmills, and, um, graffiti-covered bicycle tunnels.






Also: maybe this should have been obvious, but Munch was a much better artist than van Dongen.
