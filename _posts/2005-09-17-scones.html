--- layout: post title: 'Scones' date: 2005-09-17 07:53:00 tags: [food, corporatization, ireland] --- Real scones were provided at the first morning tea and coffee break at Graph Drawing. The rest of the breaks had biscuits instead, so I pined after the scones for the rest of the conference. The plastic-wrapped rubber imitations in the dorm breakfast baskets did not satisfy, though I saw someone else bought some to bring back. Why is it so difficult to find real scones in the US? And why do people think those baking-powder-flavored chunks of concrete you can get at Starbucks in any way resemble scones?
