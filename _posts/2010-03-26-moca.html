--- layout: post title: 'MOCA' date: 2010-03-26 19:06:00 tags: [architecture, art, lombardi, photography] --- Yesterday I went up to L.A. to see MOCA, and its sister museum the Geffen Contemporary a short walk away. I came back with some pictures of art and of the nearby Disney Hall.

I don't usually take or keep photos of individual 2d artworks (what could I add as a photographer to what's already there?) but I wanted a copy of the Gabriel Orozco print below. The blend of organic and geometric forms appeals to me, and (despite the lack of conspiracy theories) his use of line reminds me a bit of Mark Lombardi.
